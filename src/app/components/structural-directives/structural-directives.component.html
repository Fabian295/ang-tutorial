Structural Directives

Structural Directives are identified by the asterisk at the beginning of each Structural Directive. *ngFor is the equivalent for the Vanilla-JS "for of" loop. Unlike in JavaScript (JS), where the for of loop can only be used for objects, can *ngFor be used for both objects and arrays.
